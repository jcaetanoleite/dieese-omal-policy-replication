## IBGE

This category includes, among other files:

- POF 2017–2018 microdata and supporting documentation;
- PNAD Contínua tables and extracts;
- IPCA and SIDRA tables;
- geographic files and methodological documentation produced by IBGE.

Provider: Instituto Brasileiro de Geografia e Estatística — IBGE.

## Ipea

This category includes minimum-wage and other economic series obtained from Ipeadata.

Provider: Instituto de Pesquisa Econômica Aplicada — Ipea.

## DIEESE

This category includes:

- the basic food-basket series;
- the necessary minimum-wage series; and
- DIEESE methodological documents.

Provider: Departamento Intersindical de Estatística e Estudos Socioeconômicos — DIEESE.
